My Story

I'm a pixel whisperer and code poet. As both designer and developer, I translate human needs into digital experiences that feel effortless where every interaction, from a button click to a content layout, just makes sense without explanation. My superpower? Seeing the connective tissue between clean code and emotional design. You'll find me:
• Writing React components with the same care as UX copy
• Debating ideal button states over (very) strong coffee
• Collecting urban textures and weird typefaces for inspiration
I believe technology works best when it respects people's time, intelligence, and emotions whether that's through thoughtful error states, inclusive design patterns, or interfaces that load faster than you can blink. (Current obsessions: Design tokens, variable fonts, and the perfect 9AM playlist for debugging focus.) Let's build interfaces that don't just function, but feel right.
